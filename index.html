About Ugarte Legal Firm

With over 31 years of dedicated service in immigration law, Ugarte Legal Firm was founded with one clear mission: to advocate for individuals and families navigating the complexities of U.S. immigration. I, Attorney Ugarte, have spent decades standing beside clients from all walks of life, providing honest, fair, and transparent legal representation.

My deep expertise in immigration law is matched by a passion for justice and equity. I believe every client deserves not just representation, but advocacy — someone who will fight for their dreams and their future.

Clients who walk through our doors leave empowered, informed, and confident. If I represent you, rest assured: your case will be handled with integrity, skill, and a commitment to results.
